Privacy Notice

Effective July 22, 2026. This notice explains the limited information collected through the Everlume research-inquiry form.

Information collected

The inquiry form may collect your name, organization, email address, material of interest, intended research use, acknowledgment, and message.

How information is used

Inquiry information is used to review and respond to requests, confirm availability or documentation, evaluate the stated research purpose, maintain operational records, prevent misuse, and protect the website.

Information not requested

Do not submit medical records, health information, government identifiers, passwords, banking details, payment-card information, or other sensitive personal information through the inquiry form.

Service providers

The website and inquiry form are hosted through Netlify. Information may be processed by hosting, security, email, or other operational providers used to deliver and protect the service.

Retention and security

Information should be retained only as reasonably necessary for inquiry review, records, security, and applicable obligations. No internet transmission or storage method can be guaranteed completely secure.

Your request

You may use the website inquiry channel to ask about information associated with a prior inquiry. Everlume may need to verify the request before responding.

Effective for the controlled preview version of this website. This notice should be updated if analytics, payments, accounts, advertising pixels, or additional data collection are introduced.
