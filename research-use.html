Research-Use Policy

This policy describes Everlume’s intended research-only posture and the information expected during an availability inquiry.

Intended purpose

Materials presented by Everlume are intended solely for legitimate laboratory research and educational investigation. They are not offered for human or veterinary consumption, diagnosis, treatment, mitigation, cure, or prevention of disease.

Prohibited uses

Materials must not be used as food, drugs, cosmetics, household products, or medical products. Everlume does not provide dosing, administration, injection, or personal-use instructions.

Inquiry review

Everlume may request the purchaser’s name, organization, intended research use, destination, and other information reasonably necessary to evaluate an inquiry. Submission does not guarantee availability or acceptance.

Documentation and handling

Available identity, quantity, storage, handling, or related documentation should be reviewed before acquisition or use. The researcher is responsible for appropriate facilities, training, controls, storage, and disposal.

No medical claims

Product names, categories, and research descriptions do not constitute medical advice or representations of safety, efficacy, approval, or fitness for a personal or clinical purpose.

This operational policy is provided for transparency and does not replace requirements that may apply to a purchaser, product, transaction, or jurisdiction.
